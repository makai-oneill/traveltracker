---

**TravelTracker** is a sophisticated iOS app for explorers, travelers, and geography enthusiasts. Track your journeys across countries and U.S. states/territories with an interactive and visually engaging interface.

---

## Features

- **Region Tracking:** Mark the countries and U.S. states/territories you’ve visited with precise visual indicators.  
- **Interactive Logs:** Explore your travel history in organized lists or a dynamic grid of flags and icons.  
- **High-Quality Visuals:** Vector-based flags and region icons ensure crisp, scalable graphics for every screen.  
- **Modern Interface:** Sleek, clean design optimized for dark mode and intuitive navigation.  
- **Progress Visualization:** Quickly see your global adventures and monitor the regions you’ve covered.  
- **Personal Notes:** Optionally add dates, experiences, or companions for each trip, keeping your memories structured and accessible.  

---

## Why TravelTracker

TravelTracker turns every trip into a **comprehensive visual record**, helping you:

- Visualize your global travels at a glance.  
- Plan future adventures efficiently.  
- Engage with geography in a structured, interactive way.  

Perfect for frequent travelers, students, educators, or anyone passionate about exploring the world.
