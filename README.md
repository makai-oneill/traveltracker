**TravelTracker** is a sleek and intuitive iOS app designed for explorers, travelers, and geography enthusiasts who want to track the places they’ve visited. Whether you’re visiting countries across the globe or states within the U.S., TravelTracker provides a visual and interactive way to log your adventures.

With TravelTracker, you can:

- Keep track of **visited countries** and **U.S. states/territories** with detailed logs.
- Add personal **notes** about each trip, such as dates, experiences, and companions.
- View your travel history in **organized lists** or a **grid of flags and icons**.
- Enjoy a **clean, modern interface** that adapts beautifully to dark mode.
- Experience **high-quality visuals** with vector flags and region icons for crisp, scalable graphics.

TravelTracker makes it easy to see at a glance which countries and states you’ve visited, encouraging more adventures and helping you plan future travels. Perfect for frequent travelers, students, or anyone who loves geography and wants a fun, interactive way to track their journey.
